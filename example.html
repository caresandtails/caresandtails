Who we are

Cares&Tails is a brand focused on helping families and people with their dogs. Created by three young women, it offers a solution to the issues that arise when needing to leave your dog on its own.

It is a brand focused on community building and companionship. Aiming to help fellow dog lovers in the wonderful, but at times challenging, journey of owning a dog.

Our vision

Cares&Tails vision is to give our customers peace of mind while leaving their dogs when most needed. We strive to help solve the problem many people face when leaving home for extended periods of time and facing the difficulty of having no one to look after their pets. Through Care&Tails we aim to revolutionize the dog owner's community and help them by introducing our brand.

In a world where loneliness has never been as prominent as now, we believe that true connections can help fight it. Only then can we move to a world where bondsand companionship has never been stronger.
